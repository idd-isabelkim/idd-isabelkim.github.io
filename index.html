    About Our Study

    Interaction Design, in George Brown College, lead us to the world of interdisciplinary field of design skills for user experience, interface development and graphic design with coding fundamentals. Through the study, we are able to create digital platforms focusing on the ways users will be interacting.

    Modified by the psychological, social, economic and cultural factors that shape our present and future world, this ever-evolving field envisions new ways of humancomputer interaction within systems, services, products and spaces across multiple industries.

    Throughout the program, we were expected to address such topics as mixed realities, immersive environments, usability, spacial relations and human factors. We engaged in variety of research, design, testing and group work to create original interactions in a way that is meaningful and practical.
